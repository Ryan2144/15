How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with number incremented by 1!

Note: This site is another version of 15, using a 6x6 grid.

Caution: Higher integers may spawn, even though there is a lower chance of spawning higher integers. In addition, 2 tiles will spawn each move.

Other modes: Normal mode Undo mode
